Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lily who loved to play with her toys. One day, she found a big, red ball in her room. She was so happy and wanted to play with it all day.
Lily took the ball outside to the park. She saw a boy named Tom playing with a toy car. Tom was playing with the car when he accidentally dropped it. Lily was very sad and started to cry. Tom felt bad and wanted to help Lily.
Tom said, "Don't cry, Lily. I will help you." He picked up the ball and gave it back to Lily. She was so happy and thanked Tom. They played with the ball together and had lots of fun. From that day on, Lily and Tom became best friends, and they always played with the red ball.
