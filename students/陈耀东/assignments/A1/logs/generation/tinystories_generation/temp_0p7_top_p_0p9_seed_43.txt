Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lily who loved to play with her toy blocks. One day, she wanted to build a big tower with her blocks. She started to put one block on top of another, making the tower taller and taller.
As she was playing, her little brother Tom came into the room. He saw the tower and wanted to play with it too. But Tom didn't want to share the tower. He said, "No, this tower is mine!" Lily felt sad and asked, "Please, Tom, just for a little bit."
Tom thought about it and decided to share the tower with Lily. They both played with the blocks together, building the tower even taller. In the end, they learned that sharing made them both happy and having fun.
The moral of the story is that sharing and being kind can make others happy and create new friendships.
