Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lily who loved to draw. She had a big box of crayons that she loved to use every day. One day, she found a new color in her box. It was a very pretty color. Lily wanted to make her room look even more nice.
Lily started to draw on the walls of her new crayons. She drew a big sun, a house, and a happy family. But then, she made a mistake. She tried to draw on the walls, but she made a big mess. The colors went all over the floor. Lily felt ashamed because she did not mean to make a mess.
Lily's mom saw the mess and was not happy. She told Lily that she should not draw on the walls. Lily felt bad for making a mess. She promised her mom that she would clean up the mess. Lily learned her lesson and promised to be more careful. From that day on, she only drew on paper and made sure she did not draw on the walls.
