Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lily who loved to draw. She had a big box of crayons that she loved to colour with. One day, Lily wanted to draw a picture of her favorite things: a purple crayon.
Lily asked her mom if she could use the purple crayon to draw a big, happy sun. Her mom said, "Of course, Lily! Let's get started!" They started to draw the sun on the wall with a big smile on it.
As they were drawing, something unexpected happened. The purple crayon started to move on its own! It turned into a beautiful purple crayon. Lily and her mom were very surprised. They kept drawing more and more things with the purple crayon, making a beautiful rainbow. And from that day on, the crayon became their favorite colors.
